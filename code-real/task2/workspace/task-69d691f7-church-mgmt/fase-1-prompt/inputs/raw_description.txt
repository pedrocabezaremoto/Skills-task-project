Build a management platform for churches or community organizations to coordinate small groups, member involvement, and events. The organization registers with a name, mission statement, and administrative staff. Member profiles include name, contact information, family connections (spouse, children linked as separate profiles), membership date, and involvement areas tagged from a configurable list (worship team, youth ministry, hospitality, outreach, etc.). Small groups are created with a name, description, meeting frequency, meeting day and time, location, leader assigned from the membership, and a category (Bible study, support group, service team, social). Members browse available groups and request to join, with the group leader approving requests. Each group has a roster, an attendance tracker where leaders record who attended each meeting, a discussion board for group communication, and a prayer request list visible only to group members. The events calendar shows church-wide events with date, time, location, description, and RSVP functionality where members indicate whether they're attending. A volunteer scheduling system for recurring roles (like Sunday service volunteers) creates weekly schedules by slot and position, allowing members to sign up or be assigned, with conflict detection when someone is double-booked and a gap alert when positions are unfilled. A check-in system for events records attendance with a simple name-search and check-in button interface. A giving tracker records donations per member (amount, date, fund designation like general, missions, or building) with annual summaries for each member and organization-wide totals. A communication tool lets staff send announcements to all members or filtered by involvement area. Reporting shows group health metrics like average attendance trends and member engagement scores based on event and group participation frequency. All data persists in SQLite with JWT authentication supporting staff, group leader, and member roles.
